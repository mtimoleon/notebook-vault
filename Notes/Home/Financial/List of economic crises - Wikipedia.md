## 7th century[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=List_of_economic_crises&action=edit&section=3)]

[Coin exchange crisis of 692](https://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%94%D0%B2%D0%B0%D0%B4%D1%86%D0%B0%D1%82%D0%B8%D0%BB%D0%B5%D1%82%D0%BD%D1%8F%D1%8F_%D0%B0%D0%BD%D0%B0%D1%80%D1%85%D0%B8%D1%8F#%D0%9C%D0%BE%D0%BD%D0%B5%D1%82%D0%BD%D1%8B%D0%B9_%D0%BA%D1%80%D0%B8%D0%B7%D0%B8%D1%81_692_%D0%B3%D0%BE%D0%B4%D0%B0). Byzantine emperor [Justinian II](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Justinian_II) refuses to accept tribute from the [Umayyad Caliphate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Umayyad_Caliphate) with new Arab gold coins for fear of exposing double counting in the Byzantine financial system (actual weight less, than nominal quantity), which leads to the [Battle of Sebastopolis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Sebastopolis) and the revolt of taxpayers who burned financial officials in a [copper bull](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Forum_of_the_Ox). Justinian II was tortured by cutting off his nose in front of spectators at the Hippodrome. [Twenty Years' Anarchy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Twenty_Years%27_Anarchy) begins.

## 17th century[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=List_of_economic_crises&action=edit&section=5)]

- [Kipper und Wipper](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kipper_und_Wipper) (1618–22) financial crisis at the start of the Thirty Years' War
- [Tulip mania](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tulip_mania) (1637) an [economic bubble](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Economic_bubble) that burst, hurting the economy of the Dutch Republic
- [The General Crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_General_Crisis) (1640) Arguably the largest worldwide crisis in history[_opinion__]_

## 18th century[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=List_of_economic_crises&action=edit&section=6)]

- [Great Tobacco Depression](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Great_Tobacco_Depression&action=edit&redlink=1) (1703) (British America)[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_economic_crises#cite_note-:0-2)
- [South Sea Bubble](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Sea_Bubble) (1720) (UK)
- [Mississippi Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi_Company) (1720) (France)
- [Crisis of 1763](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amsterdam_banking_crisis_of_1763) – started in Amsterdam, begun by the collapse of [Leendert Pieter de Neufville](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leendert_Pieter_de_Neufville) and [Johann Ernst Gotzkowsky](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johann_Ernst_Gotzkowsky), spread to Germany and Scandinavia
- [Great East Indian Bengal Bubble Crash](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bengal_Bubble_of_1769) (1769) (India) Crash started by the rapid overvaluation of the [East India Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_India_Company).
- [Crisis of 1772](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crisis_of_1772) – started in London and Amsterdam, begun by the collapse of the bankers Neal, James, Fordyce, and Down.
- [War of American Independence Financing Crisis](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=War_of_American_Independence_Financing_Crisis&action=edit&redlink=1) (1776) (United States) – The French monarchy went deeply into debt to finance its 1.4 billion livre support for the colonial rebels; Spain invested 700 million reales.[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_economic_crises#cite_note-:0-2)
- Panic of 1785 – United States
- [Panic of 1792](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Panic_of_1792) – United States
- [Panic of 1796–1797](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Panic_of_1796%E2%80%931797) – Britain and United States

- [Great Depression of British Agriculture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Depression_of_British_Agriculture) (1873–1896)
- [Long Depression](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Long_Depression) (1873–1896)

    - [Panic of 1884](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Panic_of_1884)
    - [Panic of 1890](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Panic_of_1890)
    - [Panic of 1893](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Panic_of_1893), a US recession with bank failures
    - [Australian banking crisis of 1893](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australian_banking_crisis_of_1893)
    - [Panic of 1896](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Panic_of_1896)
